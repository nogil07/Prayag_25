Idea Pitch

Mission Briefing

A contest for young innovators to display their groundbreaking ideas and solutions to real-world issues. Participants will present their concepts to a panel of judges, focusing on innovation, feasibility, and impact.

Rules of Engagement

  • Team size: 1-3 members.
  • Presentation time: 5 minutes + 2 minutes for Q&A.
  • Submissions must be original ideas.
  • A slide deck (PPT/PDF) is mandatory.
