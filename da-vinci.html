Da Vinci

Mission Briefing

An exciting UI/UX design competition to unleash creativity and design thinking. Participants will be given a problem statement and must design a prototype for a web or mobile application.

Rules of Engagement

  • Individual participation.
  • Tool of choice: Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch.
  • Participants must submit a link to their interactive prototype.
  • Judging criteria: UX, UI, Innovation, and Presentation.
