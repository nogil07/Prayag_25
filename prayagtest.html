About The Fest

Prayag '25 is not just a tech fest; it's a nationwide confluence of brilliant minds, groundbreaking ideas, and technological marvels. Hosted in the historic city of Prayagraj, we aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application, fostering a culture of innovation and collaboration.

Innovate

Integrate

Fostering collaboration between different engineering disciplines, academia, and the tech industry.

Inspire

Igniting passion and curiosity through talks from industry leaders and hands-on workshops on cutting-edge tech.

FAQ

Who can participate in Prayag '25?

Prayag '25 is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students from any recognized college or university. Some events may have specific prerequisites, so please check the event details page.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, there is a nominal base registration fee to attend the fest. Individual events and workshops may have separate fees. You can find all the details on our registration portal.

Will accommodation be provided for outstation participants?

Yes, we will provide accommodation on a first-come, first-served basis within the college hostels for a minimal charge. We recommend booking your spot early as availability is limited.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Absolutely! You can participate in as many events as you like, as long as the event timings do not clash. We encourage you to explore a variety of challenges.
